WHAT WE DONano Pill Bug

We designed "Nano Pill Bug",
the DNA origami robot that aims to capture and protect non-modified miRNA.

INTRODUCTION

  • miRNA-Based Cancer Diagnosis

    Practical application of miRNA for early diagnosis of cancer is just around the corner.

  • "Modified miRNA"

    Many of the miRNAs in blood are "modified miRNA" that are resistant to RNase and are protected by proteins and liposomes.

  • Elucidation of the full function of miRNA

    To get the full picture of miRNAs, we need to protect "non-modified miRNA".
